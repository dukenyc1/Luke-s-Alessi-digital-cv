About Luke Alessi

Co-founded Students Against Vaping E-cigarettes (SAVe) to educate young people about the dangers of flavored e- cigarettes & advocate for the regulation of flavored e-cigarettes and the predatory behavior of e-cigarette companies towards teens.www.StudentsAgainstVaping.org 2018 – 2021 - Interview with various networks and media sites, including CNBC, NBC News, NY1, Good Morning America, and Complex to discuss the dangers of teenage vaping and provide a teenager’s perspective 10/2020- 2021 – Launched #VapesAreTrash campaign to target the environmental harms of e-cigarette waste; Created Instagram contest to win sustainable products; www.vapesaretrash.org 07/2019 – Attended NYC Mayor DeBlasio’s signing into law a complete flavor ban on all e-cigarettes and vaping products. This was a result of our testifying in NYC Council Health Committee hearing in early 2019 2018 - Created & launched the SAVe website; created original logo 12/2018 - Met with 19 members of the FDA in Washington D.C. to present the teen perspective on vaping; informed the FDA about the struggles teens faced today with nicotine addiction due to flavored vapes; focused on marketing and providing suggestions on how they can support this population
